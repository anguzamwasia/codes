Maize Performance In Trans-Nzoia

Trans-Nzoia County, Kenya's "breadbasket," excels in maize production due to fertile soils and favorable climate. However, challenges like pests, climate change, and input costs affect yields. Efforts such as improved seed varieties and sustainable farming enhance performance, making maize a cornerstone for food security and economic stability in the region.
